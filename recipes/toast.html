Toast

Description

A simple yet delicious breakfast or snack option, toast is made by browning slices of bread until crispy and golden, and then topping with your favorite spreads or toppings.

Ingredients

  • Bread slices (white, whole grain, sourdough, or your choice)
  • Butter or margarine (optional)
  • Your favorite spreads or toppings (jam, peanut butter, avocado, honey, etc.)

Steps

  1. Place slices of bread into a toaster, or on a baking sheet if using an oven.
  2. Toast the bread until it reaches your desired level of crispiness and color.
  3. Remove the toast carefully from the toaster or oven.
  4. Spread butter or your chosen topping evenly over the surface of the toast while it is still warm.
  5. Serve immediately and enjoy!
