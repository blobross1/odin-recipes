Brownies

Description

Rich, fudgy brownies are a classic dessert made with chocolate, butter, sugar, eggs, and flour. Perfect for satisfying any sweet tooth!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup (115g) unsalted butter
  • 1 cup (200g) granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/3 cup (40g) unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup (65g) all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking powder
  • Optional: 1/2 cup (90g) chocolate chips or chopped nuts

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour an 8x8-inch baking dish or line it with parchment paper.
  2. In a microwave-safe bowl, melt the butter. Stir in sugar, eggs, and vanilla extract until well combined.
  3. Mix in cocoa powder, flour, salt, and baking powder until the batter is smooth. If using, fold in chocolate chips or nuts.
  4. Pour the batter into the prepared baking dish, spreading it evenly.
  5. Bake for 20 to 25 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out with a few moist crumbs.
  6. Let the brownies cool in the pan for about 10 minutes, then transfer to a wire rack to cool completely before cutting into squares.
  7. Serve and enjoy!
